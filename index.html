Before I started learning about web development, I worked as a chef for 15 years. During that time, I had the privilege to work in some of the best kitchens in the country.

A few years ago, I started learning Python programming in my free time. After developing my first program, I realized that I'd found a new passion. I love the process of bringing websites and web applications to life.

When I'm not studying, I enjoy cooking (obviously) and playing music. I also enjoy learning more about the world around me, so I'm always listening to podcasts, reading, and traveling when I can.
